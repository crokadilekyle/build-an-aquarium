Catch a Brain Rot

Grab roaming brainrots, dodge Ohio Fog, store the good ones, and stack Sigma income while you stay alive.

  • Move with the virtual joystick on mobile, or `WASD` / arrow keys on desktop.
  • Run into roaming brainrots to catch them, then carry them into the Sigma Zone.
  • Tap stored lucky blocks to open them, and tap the trade button or press `T` to trade a stored brainrot.
  • Use the circular drop button on mobile or press `Q` on desktop to drop what you are carrying.
